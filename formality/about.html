Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why?

Because we can. Our mission is to offer a fully free and open Dynamic DNS service for all.

How is this free?

From our generous donors and out of our own pockets. subte.cc is also built to use as little resources as possible in order to minimize operating costs.

What about my data?

We only store email data and IP assignments, token and passwords are hashed. We cannot look at them even if we wanted to. Emails are only used once on account creation for verification.
