subte.cc Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what information subte.cc ("the Service") collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

subte.cc is a dynamic DNS (DDNS) service that allows users to assign subdomains to IP addresses they control. The Service is independently operated on privately managed infrastructure, including virtual private servers.

By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service means you accept any updates.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate and secure the Service.

Account Data

  • Email address
  • Password hash (never stored in plaintext)
  • Account creation date
  • Last login timestamp

Service Data

  • Registered subdomains
  • Assigned IPv4 and IPv6 addresses
  • Update/API tokens
  • Domain activity status

Technical and Security Data

  • Login IP addresses
  • API request IP addresses
  • User agent strings
  • Security and abuse logs
  • Basic request metadata

3. How We Use Data

We use collected data only to:

  • Operate the DDNS service
  • Authenticate users
  • Process DNS updates
  • Maintain security
  • Prevent abuse
  • Investigate incidents
  • Maintain system reliability

We do not:

  • Sell personal data
  • Use data for advertising
  • Track users across websites
  • Build behavioral profiles

4. Cookies and Local Storage

subte.cc may use minimal cookies or browser storage for:

  • Session authentication
  • Security protection
  • Basic website functionality

These are not used for advertising or tracking.

Disabling cookies may affect functionality.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data.

We may share limited data only when necessary:

Service Providers

Data may be processed by infrastructure providers (such as VPS or hosting providers) strictly to operate the Service.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to:

  • Comply with applicable law
  • Respond to valid legal requests
  • Enforce our Terms
  • Protect the Service or users
  • Investigate abuse

Business Transfers

If the Service is transferred or reorganized, data may be transferred as part of that process.

6. Abuse Handling

Because subte.cc provides DNS infrastructure, we may receive abuse reports.

We may investigate reports involving:

  • Phishing
  • Malware distribution
  • Botnet activity
  • Spam infrastructure
  • Network attacks
  • Fraud
  • Other illegal activity

If abuse is confirmed, we may:

  • Suspend domains
  • Disable DNS resolution
  • Suspend accounts
  • Remove data related to abuse
  • Block access to the Service

We may also preserve relevant technical logs to investigate abuse.

subte.cc only provides DNS resolution and does not host user content, but we may act when domains are used for harmful purposes.

7. Law Enforcement Requests

We may respond to lawful requests from authorities when legally required.

We may disclose limited information such as:

  • Account email
  • Registration dates
  • Domain records
  • Relevant IP logs

We attempt to:

  • Only respond to valid legal requests
  • Disclose the minimum necessary data
  • Protect user privacy where possible

We may reject requests that are overly broad or invalid.

Unless legally prohibited, we may notify affected users.

8. Security Measures

We implement reasonable safeguards such as:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Access restrictions
  • Authentication controls
  • Firewall protections
  • Logging of administrative access
  • Token authentication for DNS updates

However, no service can guarantee absolute security.

9. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary.

Typical retention:

  • Active accounts: retained while active
  • Deleted accounts: removed within a reasonable period
  • Security logs: retained for abuse prevention
  • Inactive domains: may be removed after long inactivity

Some data may be retained longer if required for legal or security reasons.

10. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access your account data
  • Correct your information
  • Delete your account
  • Delete your domains

Deleting your account removes personal data within a reasonable timeframe except where retention is required for security or legal purposes.

11. Third-Party Links

The Service may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

12. Contact

For privacy or data requests:

privacy@subte.cc

For abuse reports:

abuse@subte.cc

13. Philosophy

subte.cc follows a minimal data philosophy:

  • Collect as little data as possible
  • Keep infrastructure simple
  • Prefer transparency over complexity
  • Operate in good faith

The goal of the Service is to provide reliable DNS infrastructure, not to collect user data.
