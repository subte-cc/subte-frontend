# Subte.cc closed beta announcement

The wait is **almost** over, after some time of building, and perhaps a few too many late nights coding, we are thrilled to announce the subte.cc closed beta.

Subte.cc is a dynamic DNS service free and open source released under the GNU General Public License 2.0

Invite codes were given at events and different parts of the internet as to start beta testing of the service

## Why a closed beta?

We’ve spent the last few months laying the groundwork and building out our infrastructure. Before we open the gates for a full production release, we want to ensure everything runs smoothly. By starting with a smaller group of users, we can stress-test the system and fine-tune before opening the gates.

## How it works

Invite codes have already begun appearing at tech events and across various corners of the internet. These codes allow early adopters to jump in and help us shape the future of the service.

### Get involved

If you cant find a invite code anywhere, you can **request one** by sending a email to support (at) subte.cc


 **Note**: as this is in beta, expect the occasional "pardon our dust" moment. we recommend using the service for testing and development enviroments rather than mission-critical production systems during this period
